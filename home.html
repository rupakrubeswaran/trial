About Our Foundation

Makkalil Oruvan Foundation was started by a group of school and college friends with a shared passion for serving the underprivileged and creating a positive impact in society. Through compassion, integrity, and respect, we strive to uplift communities by providing basic necessities, supporting education, and fostering a brighter, more hopeful future for all.
